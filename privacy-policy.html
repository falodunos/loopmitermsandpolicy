PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated on February 1, 2019

LoopMi is a product of Antwire Technologies Ltd (the “Company”). This privacy statement describes how the Company protects and makes use of the information you give the Company when you use the LoopMi website, app and other services.

What Information Will We Collect​?

Personal Information may be collected, stored and used when using our app, visiting our website, registering for a trial, or making a transaction. The personal information that you are asked to provide will be made clear to you at the point we ask you to provide your personal information.

  • Information you provide:
    • When visiting our website or using our apps

      If you choose to contact us through either of these sources, information you provide such as your name, email address or telephone number and any other information you choose to provide us with will also be stored.

    • Account Registration

      When registering for an account/trial, details that you provide us with to create a user and provide you with access to the applications. The registration requires you to provide us with a means of personal identification, such as your picture, and personal contact information, such as your name, address,company name and email address.

    • Payment Information

      When paying for an order online, your financial information (i.e your payment card details and/or bank account details) is directed to our third-party payment processor. We do not store your financial information on our systems.

    • LoopMi Visitors

      If a User signs a visitor, or you are a visitor that has signed into our application, the information provided, such as the name, company, who is being visited and other information that the customer being visited requires, e.g. the number plate of your vehicle.

  • Information we collect:
    • When visiting our website or using our apps

      Information gathered by the use of cookies in your web browser, such as your IP address, geographical location, operating system and browser. We use cookies to analyse our web traffic using an analytics package. Aggregated usage data helps us improve the website structure, design, content and functions. Cookies do not provide us with access to your computer or any information about you, other than that which you choose to share with us. You can use your web browser’s cookie settings to determine how our website uses cookies. If you do not want our website to store cookies on your computer or device, you should set your web browser to refuse cookies. However, please note that doing this may affect how our website functions. Some pages and services may become unavailable to you.

For What Purpose Will We Process Your Data​?

Your data will be processed for legitimate purposes. We collect and process the personal information detailed above to:

  • provide you with the required services;
  • respond to your questions or requests;
  • improve LoopMi’s operations;
  • address inappropriate use of our website;
  • prevent, detect and manage risk against fraud and illegal activities;
  • target advertisements, newsletter and service updates;
  • verify the information that you provide with third parties;
  • to administer a contest, promotion, survey or other site feature;
  • to send periodic emails;
  • update our database, improve content and website layout; and
  • resolve disputes that may arise.

With your consent, we may also collect additional personal information in other ways including emails, surveys, and other forms of communication. Once you begin using our services through your LoopMi account we will keep records of your data inputs, transactions and collect information of your other activities related to our services. We will not share or disclose your Personal Information with a third party without your consent.

Some messages from us are service-related and necessary for customers. You agree that we can send you non-marketing emails or messages, such as those related to transactions, your account, security, or product changes/updates.

Note: If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, we include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email.

How Do We Protect Your Information?

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. We offer the use of a secure server. All supplied sensitive information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption technology to safeguard your Personal Information. Other security safeguards include but are not limited to data encryption, firewalls, and physical access controls to our building and files and only granting access to Personal Information to only employees who require it to fulfil their job responsibilities.

Do We Use Cookies?

Yes - please refer to 2(a) above. In addition to 2(a) above, we use cookies to identify you as a User and make your user experience easier, customise our services, content and advertising; help you ensure that your account security is not compromised, mitigate risk and prevent fraud; and to promote trust and safety on our website. Cookies allow our servers to remember your account log-in information when you visit our website, IP addresses, date and time of visits, monitor web traffic and prevent fraudulent activities. If your browser or browser add-on permits, you have the choice to disable cookies on our website, however this may limit your ability to use our website.
